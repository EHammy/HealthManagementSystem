Help

Getting Started

Here are some tips for getting started with our application:

  • Make sure you have a reliable internet connection
  • Create an account if you haven't already
  • Explore the various features and sections of the application
  • Contact support if you have any questions or issues

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some answers to our most commonly asked questions:

How do I change my password?
You can change your password by going to the settings page and selecting the "Change Password" option.
How do I add a new entry?
To add a new entry, go to the corresponding section of the application (e.g. "Add New Vitals" for adding new vitals) and fill out the form.
How do I view my past entries?
You can view your past entries by going to the corresponding section of the application and selecting the "View All" option.
